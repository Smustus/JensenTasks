Card Turner Simulator

Fulfil your dream of becoming a card turning master!

Enter a Time to determine how long it takes for every card to reset, then enter an integer between 1-52 to generate the corresponding amount of random cards from a deck

These cards can then be shuffled around and/or sorted according to suit and rank according to preference

Your goal is to turn all the cards around before they reset!

Great game if you're a reptile, Have fun!
